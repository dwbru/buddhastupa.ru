About

In documentary "Stupas of Miracles" we will tell about the Buddhist stupas which are the symbol of absolute human mind development — the Enlightenment, and also serve for peace and harmony safeguarding on Earth.

You will know about the stupas' symbolism, their construction and filling guidelines, this tradition origination history and about the miracles which have been taken and still take place near the stupas.

We will visit the first stupas in India and Nepal, Buryatia and Kalmykia. We will meet the Lamas who will tell us about the stupas' meaning for their people.

You will see the new stupas constructed in Poland, Czech Republic, Germany, Greece, Spain, Russia and other countries.

Загрузка видео...

We will find out from the builders and designers of stupas why do they put their power, money and time with such enthusiasm into the projects of these installations construction.

«Stupas are the monuments preserving peace in the world. Through their perfect form, these structures express the clear nature of mind — the Enlightenment» Manfred Seegers
